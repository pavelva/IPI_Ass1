What Is Web 3?

Lets say that you want to go out, have dinner, visit a soccer game and you don't own a car. Searching all the relevant information including restaurants, soccer games, ticket prices and transportation might take a lot of time. Now think that your web browser would do all the research for you and it will only supply the final answers that fit your personal preferences. Now imagen that the browser will also manage to learn your preferences with time while you use it. But wait, what if you will open your tablet or your phone and it will know your preferences as well even though this is your first time using it. This is web 3!

Answering what is Web 3 is hard' but the main idea is that there will be no more web pages but information. The information will be available as information and not as web pages or other type of specific content. It wont matter if you are using you PC or your tablet, the information will be available and you "information agents" will do all the work for you, meaning searching in the sea of data, filtering the data the way you would have filltered it and present the data in the best way suitable for you and you only.

This is obviously the next big thing, but implementing it will be hard and will make us replace all our machines.
